📘 Quick Lesson: What is a Verb?

✅ Verb

A verb is an action word.

Examples: run, eat, read, write, jump

❌ Noun

A noun is a person, place, animal or thing.

Examples: teacher, school, book, cat

❌ Adjective

An adjective describes a noun.

Examples: happy, tall, beautiful, small
